What is PantryMeals?

PantryMeals is the only platform that help you quickly decide on what to cook with the ingredients you already have in your fridge and pantry. Our recipe library has over 350,000 ingredients and includes any recipe you want to cook. With the PantryMeals mobile app you can add your ingredients in your pantry account profile so the app can help you decide on the best meals and cuisines you can cook right away with the ingredients you already have in your pantry profile. Do you enjoy cooking? Make it a hobby. The PantryMeals app is the first of its kind, our Android and IOS app, and website is a food focused social network cooking and recipe platform where everyone is a chef. You can create recipes by sharing step-by-step instructions for your followers, list all the ingredients of the recipe and build your own library of recipes. Save your favorite recipes under your user profile to find them quickly a later time or for future use. Find all the best recipes from our massive library, share your favorite dishes from your chef profile, connect with foodies and other chef enthusiasts to create the best recipes and cultural cuisines found on the web. The PantryMeals app is available for both IOS and Android and you can also browse our recipe library from any internet web browser.

How do I use PantryMeals?

Have some ingredients at home and not sure what you can make? Just add your ingredients in “Pantry” under your user profile and the PantryMeals app will help you find the best recipes you can make instantly with those ingredients. No matter what ingredients you have PantryMeals will help you discover amazing recipes from its massive recipe library.

Going to the grocery store or market can be a hassle. With the PantryMeals android and IOS app you can stay organized with your grocery list. When adding items into your shopping list, just type the first few letters of an ingredient and the PantryMeals app will help you find the ingredient you are looking for to add items into your shopping list. Find a recipe from our platform you would love to make but don’t have the ingredients? You can also add or build your shopping list from your favorite recipes by clicking on any ingredient and click add to “shopping list”. After you complete your shopping you can instantly click “add to panty” from the shopping list so the app can help you discover recipes you can make with the ingredients you have in your “pantry”.

The PantryMeals app is the only food and recipe app you will ever need for all your cooking needs. Download our app for free from the apple app store and the android play store.
